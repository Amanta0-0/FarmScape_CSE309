Detailed Growth Plan

Tomato Growth Optimization Plan

Current Stage: Fruiting

Your tomatoes are in the fruiting stage, which requires specific care to maximize yield and quality.

Fertilization Schedule

  • Today: Apply phosphorus-rich fertilizer (5-10-5 ratio)
  • Weekly: Apply compost tea to boost microbial activity
  • In 2 weeks: Side-dress with well-rotted manure

Watering Instructions

During fruiting stage, maintain consistent moisture:

  • Frequency: Daily watering
  • Amount: 1.5L per plant
  • Timing: Early morning (avoid wetting foliage)

Pruning Guide

Proper pruning improves air circulation and directs energy to fruit production:

  • Remove leaves below the first fruit cluster
  • Prune suckers (side shoots) that appear in leaf axils
  • Remove any yellowing or diseased leaves

Pest Monitoring

Check weekly for these common issues:

  • Hornworms: Handpick and destroy
  • Aphids: Spray with water or insecticidal soap
  • Early blight: Remove affected leaves, improve air circulation

Expected Harvest Timeline

Based on current growth, expect first harvest in 10-14 days.

Harvest tomatoes when they are fully colored but still firm.
